GLD2O — Discovering the Workplace

Course Overview

This course helps you discover workplace essential skills and work habits needed for success. You'll explore careers that interest you, learn how to manage yourself and work with others, and make plans for your future learning and work.

Teacher: Mr. Chan

Format: Asynchronous Online • Grade 10 • Ontario Curriculum

Duration: ~17 instructional weeks

Submissions: All assignments except the last culminating project are completed in Pika.

Portfolio sharing: At the end of the course, your portfolio will be shared with the class.

Course Conduct

Assignments

Unit 1: Getting Started

A1
Digital Scavenger Hunt
Practice screenshots and try an AI tool.
5% • Wk 1
A2
Personal Interest Proposal
Propose a personal project you'll track through the course.
10% • Wk 2

Unit 2: Skills for Success

A3
Essential Skills
Explore 3 workplace essential skills including Digital Skills/AI.
5% • Wk 3
A4
Self-Management & Teamwork
Reflect on how you manage your time and work with others.
10% • Wk 4–5

Unit 3: Knowing Yourself & Exploring Careers

A5
Self-Assessment
Take the 16Personalities test and reflect on the results.
5% • Wk 6
A6
Career Exploration
Research 2 occupations, pathways, barriers, and health & safety.
10% • Wk 7–8

Unit 4: Planning & Preparing

A7
Decision-Making & Barriers
Use a decision matrix to evaluate options and plan for barriers.
10% • Wk 9–10
A8
Career Plan & Workplace Awareness
Create a 2–3 year plan and learn about workplace rights and issues.
5% • Wk 11
A9
Job Search Documents
Build a resume and cover letter using AI, plus interview prep.
10% • Wk 12–13

Unit 5: Showcase

A10
Final Portfolio
Build a Google Site showcasing all your work from the course.
30% • Wk 14–17
