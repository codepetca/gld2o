# Career Exploration Mega-Assignment

**Purpose**: investigate multiple occupations and decide which ones fit you best.

**Learning goals**  
- Use an inquiry process to research education and work opportunities.  
- Identify essential skills, duties, and requirements for different roles.  
- Reflect on fit, pathways, and barriers.

## What to do (complete in the assigned Google Doc)
1) Choose 2–3 occupations you genuinely want to learn about.  
2) Research each role (use at least two sources per role). Record: key duties, essential skills, typical education/training, and one local or online opportunity.  
3) Pathways and preparation: list courses, certifications, or experiences that would prepare you (school, community, online).  
4) Fit + barriers: for each role, note what excites you, what worries you, and 1–2 barriers with strategies to overcome them (e.g., cost, distance, entry requirements).  
5) Summary: pick your current top choice and explain why (1 paragraph).

## Deliverable
- The assigned Google Doc with headings for each occupation and a short summary section completed.

## How to submit
- Attach/turn in your Doc in Google Classroom.  
- Daily: log what you worked on in Pika (1–2 sentences).

## Success criteria (universal /15 rubric)
- **Completion**: research for each role, pathways, barriers, and summary are present with sources named.  
- **Thinking/communication**: clear, specific notes; shows comparison and reasoning.  
- **Workflow/authenticity**: organized notes, citations/links to sources, daily Pika check-ins.
