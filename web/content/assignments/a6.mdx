# Career Plan & Job Search Package

**Purpose**: create a short-term career plan and prepare job-search documents.

**Learning goals**  
- Plan courses, activities, and next steps that align with your goals.  
- Develop a resume and cover letter targeted to a specific opportunity.  
- Follow job-search instructions and show professional communication.

## What to do (complete in the assigned Google Doc + attach files)
1) **Career plan (1 page)**: outline your next 2–3 years. Include: target pathway, key high school courses, certifications/experiences to pursue, and one backup plan.  
2) **Opportunity summary**: pick one real posting (part-time, summer, volunteer, or entry-level). Paste the key requirements and link.  
3) **Resume**: draft a tailored resume for that posting (keep it to one page).  
4) **Cover letter**: write a one-page letter to the employer; include why you’re interested, how you meet the top requirements, and your next step.  
5) **Reflection (5–7 sentences)**: what you still need to learn/gain to be competitive.

## Deliverable
- The assigned Google Doc for the plan, posting summary, and reflection. Attach your resume and cover letter (as separate Docs or within the same file if needed).

## How to submit
- Attach/turn in your Doc + resume + cover letter in Google Classroom.  
- Daily: log what you worked on in Pika (1–2 sentences).

## Success criteria (universal /15 rubric)
- **Completion**: plan, posting summary, resume, cover letter, and reflection are present.  
- **Thinking/communication**: documents are targeted to the posting; writing is clear and professional.  
- **Workflow/authenticity**: organized files, follows instructions from the posting, daily Pika check-ins.
