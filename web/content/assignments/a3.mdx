# Self-Management & Teamwork Task

**Purpose**: build self-management routines and show how you work with others.

**Learning goals**  
- Identify strategies to manage workload, stress, and change.  
- Plan tasks using priorities and time blocking.  
- Describe effective teamwork behaviours and communication.

## What to do (complete in the assigned Google Doc)
1) **Weekly plan**: map a 5-day plan for this course + other commitments. Include: goals for the week, 3 priorities, and daily checkpoints.  
2) **Challenge response**: choose one scenario (work overload, schedule change, or conflict in a group). Describe what you would do before, during, and after to manage it (6–8 sentences).  
3) **Teamwork reflection**: describe one past teamwork experience. Note what went well, what was hard, and one strategy you will use next time (6–8 sentences).

## Deliverable
- The assigned Google Doc with all three sections completed.

## How to submit
- Attach/turn in your Doc in Google Classroom.  
- Daily: log what you worked on in Pika (1–2 sentences).

## Success criteria (universal /15 rubric)
- **Completion**: weekly plan, scenario response, and reflection are complete.  
- **Thinking/communication**: specific strategies and concrete examples; plan is realistic.  
- **Workflow/authenticity**: organized headings, clear timelines, daily Pika check-ins.
