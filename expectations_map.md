# GLD2O Expectations Map (Teacher-Facing)

Purpose: track coverage of GLD2O overall/specific expectations across assignments. Use this to confirm balance when adjusting pacing or adding activities.

## Overall Expectations Reference
- Essential Skills for Working and Learning: identify/apply essential skills; use literacy/numeracy and communication strategies; plan, assess, and document development.
- Personal Management: self-knowledge; self-management strategies; interpersonal/teamwork; task planning/organization.
- Exploration of Opportunities: research education/work opportunities; describe opportunities and preparation; understand workplace expectations/issues; follow health/safety policies.

## Assignment Coverage
- A1 Digital Scavenger Hunt (onboarding): workflow practice, tech readiness, and following instructions (low weight; supports Criterion 3 habits).
- A1B Personal Interest Proposal: Personal Management (self-knowledge, motivation, goal clarity); Exploration (initial planning of opportunities/resources).
- A2 Essential Skills Application: Essential Skills (identification, transferability, literacy/numeracy tasks; documentation of skill use).
- A3 Self-Management & Teamwork: Personal Management (self-management strategies, interpersonal/teamwork skills, task planning).
- A4 Career Exploration: Exploration of Opportunities (inquiry into occupations; education/training paths; workplace expectations).
- A5 Decision-Making & Barriers: Personal Management (decision-making process); Exploration (identifying barriers to opportunities and strategies).
- A6 Career Plan & Job Search Package: Exploration (job-search process, workplace expectations); Essential Skills (communication artifacts: resume/cover letter).
- A7 Google Site Build: Personal Management (organization, reflection); Essential Skills (document use, communication for audience).
- Final Portfolio: All three areas; emphasizes communication, organization, and synthesis of learning/evidence.

Note: Health and safety expectations can be reinforced in weekly videos or site checklists when tasks include community/job shadowing; add short reminders if you introduce those activities.
