# GLD2O Universal Rubric (/30)

## Criterion 1 — Completion of Required Components (/10)
10 – All required components completed
8 – Most components complete
6 – Some components incomplete
4 – Several parts missing
2 – Very little completed
0 – Not submitted

## Criterion 2 — Quality of Thinking & Communication (/10)
10 – Clear, thoughtful, accurate, well-explained
8 – Mostly clear; minor gaps
6 – Understandable but basic or uneven
4 – Hard to follow; weak explanations
2 – Very unclear or minimal
0 – Not submitted

## Criterion 3 — Workflow, Process & Authenticity (/10)
10 – Strong workflow; organized; followed instructions; authentic work
8 – Good workflow; mostly organized
6 – Acceptable workflow; inconsistent
4 – Weak workflow; instructions often not followed
2 – Very poor workflow; rushed; concerns about authenticity
0 – Not submitted

**Total: /30**
