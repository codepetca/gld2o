A1 Digital Scavenger Hunt

Purpose: Practice the course workflow and build the habit of daily Pika check-ins.

What to do:
1) Dino Run: Play until at least 100 points. Screenshot your score. (To play: turn off Wi-Fi, visit google.com, press spacebar.)

2) Google Trick: Search "do a barrel roll" and screenshot the spin.

3) YouTube Explorer: Search "world's shortest video," find the original famous one, and screenshot the page.

4) Confirmation: Write a sentence confirming you know how to take screenshots and paste them.

Add a short caption under each screenshot explaining what it shows.

How to submit:
- Complete this assignment in Pika.

Success criteria (/15):
- Completion: all four tasks with screenshots and captions.
- Thinking/communication: captions are clear; steps followed exactly.
- Workflow/authenticity: daily Pika check-ins, your own screenshots.
