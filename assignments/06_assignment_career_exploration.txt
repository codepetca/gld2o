A6 Career Exploration

Purpose: Research occupations and decide which ones fit you best.

What to do:
1) Choose 2 occupations you genuinely want to learn about. Consider your A5 personality results, but pick roles that interest you.

2) Research each role (use at least 2 sources per role). Record:
   - Key duties
   - Essential skills required
   - Typical education/training
   - One local or online opportunity
   - Whether it could be self-employment, contract, or flexible (remote, flex-time)

3) Pathways: List courses, certifications, or experiences that would prepare you (school, community, online).

4) Fit + barriers: For each role, note:
   - What excites you
   - What worries you
   - 1â€“2 barriers and strategies to overcome them

5) Health & safety (for your top choice):
   - One or two potential workplace hazards
   - One health & safety policy workers must follow

6) Summary (1 paragraph): Pick your top choice and explain why.

How to submit:
- Complete this assignment in Pika.

Success criteria (/15):
- Completion: research, pathways, barriers, health & safety, and summary present with sources named.
- Thinking/communication: clear notes; shows comparison and reasoning.
- Workflow/authenticity: organized, citations/links to sources, your own words.
