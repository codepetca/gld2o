Assignment 5 — Decision-Making + Barriers Task

Purpose: apply a decision-making process and plan for barriers you may face.

Learning goals:
- Use a structured decision-making or problem-solving process.
- Identify internal/external barriers and strategies to manage them.
- Communicate your reasoning clearly.

What to do (Google Doc):
1) Choose one decision related to school/work (e.g., course choice, job option, volunteer placement, skills to develop).
2) Options and criteria: list 3 options and 3–4 criteria that matter to you (cost, time, growth, interest, location, etc.).
3) Decision matrix: score each option against each criterion (simple 1–5 scale) and total the scores.
4) Barriers and strategies: name 2–3 barriers for your top option (e.g., schedule conflicts, finances, transportation) and how you would handle them.
5) Reflection (1 paragraph): which option you choose now and why; note what would make you change your mind.

Deliverable:
- One Google Doc with the matrix (table or bullets) and reflection.

How to submit:
- Attach/turn in your Doc in Google Classroom.
- Daily: log what you worked on in Pika (1–2 sentences).

Success criteria (universal /15 rubric):
- Completion: options, criteria, matrix, barriers, and reflection are all present.
- Thinking/communication: scores are explained; reasoning is clear and specific.
- Workflow/authenticity: organized layout, visible calculations, daily Pika check-ins.
