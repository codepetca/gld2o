# GLD2O Universal Rubric (Google Classroom Version)

## Criterion 1 — Completion of Required Components (/5)
**Did the student complete everything the assignment required?**

- **5** – All required components completed  
- **4** – Most components complete  
- **3** – Some components incomplete  
- **2** – Several required parts missing  
- **1** – Very little completed  
- **0** – Not submitted  

## Criterion 2 — Quality of Thinking & Communication (/5)
**Is the work clear, thoughtful, accurate, and reflective?**

- **5** – Clear, thoughtful, accurate, well-explained  
- **4** – Mostly clear; minor gaps  
- **3** – Understandable but basic or uneven  
- **2** – Hard to follow; weak explanations  
- **1** – Very unclear or minimal  
- **0** – Not submitted  

## Criterion 3 — Workflow, Process & Authenticity (/5)
**Did the student follow instructions, organize the work properly, and submit their own authentic work?**

- **5** – Strong workflow; organized; followed instructions; authentic work  
- **4** – Good workflow; mostly organized  
- **3** – Acceptable workflow; inconsistent  
- **2** – Weak workflow; instructions often not followed  
- **1** – Very poor workflow; rushed; concerns about authenticity  
- **0** – Not submitted  

## Total: /15
