# Google Site Checklist (for A7 & Final Portfolio)

- Menu has clear page names and works on mobile.
- Home page includes a short intro and “where to find things” paragraph.
- Each unit page has: overview (2–3 sentences), linked/embedded artifacts, and dates or brief context.
- At least two draft/process artifacts are included somewhere (e.g., schedule screenshot, annotated draft).
- Links and embeds open correctly for the teacher (test in an incognito window).
- Final reflection is present and specific.
